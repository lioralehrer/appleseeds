Our Story

We are Nadav & Adi, an environmental economist, and an art therapist. Parents of a cute, smiling three-year-old boy who likes to play with toys, But exhausts them after two weeks ... After struggling to adapt toys to the changing needs of our child, and with great frustration due to the economic and environmental waste of toys that stand idle, we looked for a creative solution and found it in the world of cooperative economy. Thus, PlayHouse provides a logical and simple solution for children, parents and the environment. Â 

It's really simple when you think about it...

PlayHouse" is a social-environmental project from the world of cooperative economy, which aims to provide children with a variety of quality toys, in accordance with their development, while providing professional knowledge in an easy and convenient way. The idea behind "PlayHouse" is simple - just like a library, only with toys instead of books. By purchasing a subscription to the toy library, parents can save a lot of money, reduce the mess at home, and free up essential storage space. While continuously adjusting the toys at the library to the latest needs and wishes of parent & children. Beyond the advantages for individuale families, the overall amount of toys will decrease and with it the impact on the environment.
